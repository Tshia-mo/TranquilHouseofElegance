ABOUT US

Tranquil House of Elegance, founded by Nompumelelo Tidimalo Mnisi in 2014 is an on growing Beauty Parlor that caters for most beauty needs in the industry. We operate as both a stationary and mobile business, providing excellent and professional services both at the salon or in the comfort of your own home.

Tranquil House Of Elegance is not only a Beauty Business that caters for your beauty needs but we are a brand that is uplifted by the pleasure of being "worn" by you. Our expertise create a platform for us to brand our name on every face, hairstyle, nails, etc, of each and every client that walks through our doors or invites us to theirs. We are proud that we are one of the few salons that cater for both Ethnic and Caucasian women, meeting everyone's needs with the trademark of setting trends everywhere.

We sell good quality Hair at reasonable prices and we do all this with the mentality of servicing "money wise" individuals who always sort out to looking their best. We are one of the best and equally affordable beauty parlours around, rendering services from hair, nails, massages, eyelash extensions, permanent make up and so much more.We deliver over and above your greatest expectations with great customer etiquette.
