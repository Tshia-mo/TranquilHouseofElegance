Training courses

Liquid Acrylic Training

The Liquid Acrylic Course is an Introduction to Professional Acrylic application. In this course you will learn the technical aspects of acrylic nail application done with excellence and precision. The course includes theory, practicals on 2 live models as well as Theory and Practical Assessments. We also include practical videos for you to keep practicing and improving after the training.

UV Gel Training

Our UV Gel Training is the best Gel Training, teaching you the basics on simple gel application to the complex applications from application on Tips to UV Gel Sculpting you get the best. The course includes Theory, Practical, Assessment both in theory and practical. For your continued advancement we also include 2 UV Gel application videos.

Manicure & Pedicure Training

Manicure and Pedicure are the most popular relaxation and pampering services today and is therefore why its important to have this as an added skill. Here you will learn the different types of Manicures and Pedicures from a Basic Manicure and Pedicure to a Delux/Advanced Manicure and Pedicure. Not only will you receive the techniques and skills to perform each service professionally with excellence you will also be given knowledge that will assist you better understand the importance behind a manicure and pedicure service for your client.

Combined All In One Training

In our Combined Training Course we have put together all the nail systems together. In this course you will get a complete nail experience from Acrylic nails, UV Gel nails, Manicure and Pedicure and an additional course in Acrylic and UV Gel Sculpting. You'll get the full experience with no compromise on the quality as given in the other courses
